Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI voice screening work?

Our AI system uses advanced speech recognition and natural language processing. The AI asks relevant questions, adapts based on responses, and automatically transcribes and processes the information into structured clinical data for provider review.

Is the platform HIPAA compliant?

Yes, our platform is fully HIPAA compliant with end‑to‑end encryption for all patient data. We maintain strict data privacy protocols, regular security audits, and BAA agreements with all healthcare partners.

How does this integrate with our existing EHR system?

Our platform offers seamless integration with most major EHR systems including Epic, Cerner, Allscripts, Meditech, and others. We provide REST APIs and dedicated support for custom integrations.
